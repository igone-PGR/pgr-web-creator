Our Philosophy

Roots in the Soil, Heart in the Grain.

Tierra y Trigo was born from a simple desire: to bring the authentic flavors of the Spanish countryside to the bustling streets of Madrid. We believe that a meal is only as good as its origin.

That's why we partner exclusively with local millers for our heritage flours and organic farmers for our seasonal produce. Our sourdough starter has been nurtured for five years, capturing the unique micro-flora of our neighborhood.
